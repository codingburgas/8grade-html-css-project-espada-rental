Frequently Asked Questions

How does the subscription work?
Our subscription service allows you to drive premium vehicles for a fixed monthly fee. All plans include insurance, maintenance, and roadside assistance. You can swap vehicles based on your subscription tier, giving you flexibility without the commitment of ownership.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, all our subscription plans are month-to-month. You can cancel your subscription anytime with a 7-day notice before your next billing cycle.
What happens if I exceed the mileage limit?
If you exceed your daily mileage limit, additional miles will be charged at $0.35 per mile. Our Luxury Plan includes unlimited mileage, so you don't have to worry about extra charges.
How do vehicle swaps work?
Vehicle swaps can be scheduled through our mobile app or website. Based on your subscription plan, you can exchange your current vehicle for another one from our fleet. We'll deliver the new vehicle to your preferred location and pick up the previous one.
What is included in the insurance coverage?
All plans include comprehensive insurance coverage with a deductible. Basic Plan has a $1,000 deductible, Premium Plan has a $500 deductible, and Luxury Plan has a $250 deductible. Full details are provided in the subscription agreement.
